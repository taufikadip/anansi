

 **"The Grade Calculator"**
   - **Story**: A university gives letter grades based on the following score ranges:
     - 90 and above: A
     - 80 to 89: B
     - 70 to 79: C
     - 60 to 69: D
     - Below 60: F
   - **Question**: You need to determine a student's letter grade based on their score.
